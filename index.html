Tecno Saprk 20pro

The Spark 20 Pro is built around a 6.78-inch IPS LCD panel with a 120Hz refresh rate. It also features a Dynamic Port which displays the system info status and notifications around the punch hole. Speaking of punch-hole, it houses a 32MP front camera. The device is powered by a 5000mAh battery with 33W fast charging support. Moreover, it has stereo dual speaker sound by DTS and IP53 water and dust resistance. It also boots the Android 13-based HiOS.

Infinix HOT 40 Pro

The Infinix HOT 40 Pro is an entry-level smartphone that features a 6.78-inch FHD+ display with 120Hz refresh rate, 108MP + 2MP + AI Lens triple rear cameras, and a 32MP selfie camera. It runs on a MediaTek Helio G99 processor with 8GB of RAM, Android 13 OS, and the XOS software. There's also a side-mounted fingerprint scanner, 4G LTE connectivity, dual-band Wi-Fi, and 256GB of expandable storage. A large 5,000mAh battery powers the device with 33W of fast charging technology.

Tecno Spark Go2024

The TECNO Spark Go 2024. It’s the refreshed version of its 2023 predecessor, now sporting a better chipset, a revamped design, and ‘Dynamic Island’-inspired feature. The new model equips the UNISOC T606 chipset packing improved performance (compared to its 2023 version with MediaTek Helio A22). Design-wise, it comes with a new camera module layout at the rear and the back panel appears in pastel-like colors. Its 6.6-inch HD+ display now operates at 90Hz which is a nice upgrade in its tier. Additionally, its notch is now a punch-hole (previously, dewdrop) featuring Tecno’s self-developed ‘Dynamic Port’—a quirk inspired from Apple’s Dynamic Island.

Infinix smart8

The Infinix Smart 8, a budget-friendly smartphone that combines great features with a stylish design. Let's dive into the Infinix Smart 8 specifications to understand what this device has to offer. It features a 6.6-inch display with an impressive 84.0% screen-to-body ratio, providing an immersive viewing experience. The 720 x 1612 pixel resolution and 20:9 aspect ratio result in a pixel density of approximately 267 ppi, ensuring vibrant and sharp visuals for all your content. Under the hood, the Infinix Smart 8 is powered by the Unisoc T606 chipset built on a 12nm process. Its octa-core processor, consisting of 2×1.6 GHz Cortex-A75 and 6×1.6 GHz Cortex-A55 cores, delivers reliable and efficient performance for your everyday tasks, from browsing the web to running your favorite apps. Running on Android 13, the Infinix Smart 8 provides you with the latest software features and security updates, ensuring a smooth and secure user experience. Equipped with a powerful 5000 mAh non-removable Li-Po battery, the Infinix Smart 8 ensures long-lasting usage on a single charge. You can enjoy your favorite content, stay connected, and more without worrying about running out of power during the day. In conclusion, the Infinix Smart 8 is a versatile and affordable smartphone that combines the latest Android software, capable performance, a stunning display, and reliable camera capabilities. With its impressive specifications and budget-friendly price, the Infinix Smart 8 is a smart choice for users looking for a dependable mobile device

Tecno Cammon 20ppro

The TECNO CAMON 20 Pro is a budget-friendly midrange smartphone that features a 6.67-inch FHD+ AMOLED display with a 120Hz screen refresh rate, 64MP + 2MP + AI Lens triple rear cameras with ring LED flash, and a 32 Megapixel selfie camera with its own LED flash. It runs on a MediaTek Helio G99 chipset with 8GB of RAM, Android 13 OS, and the HiOS interface. There’s also an under-display fingerprint scanner, 4G LTE connectivity, NFC, stereo dual speakers, and 256GB of expandable storage. A large 5,000mAh battery powers the device with 33W of fast charging.
